About W2G

W2G is an innovative platform that converts organic waste into biogas, empowering communities and reducing environmental impact. Our mission is to create a circular economy by incentivizing waste collection and providing affordable, clean energy.

Problem Statement

Unmanaged organic waste leads to pollution, greenhouse gas emissions, and health hazards in urban and rural areas.

Proposed Solution

W2G collects organic waste, processes it into biogas, and distributes clean energy to households and businesses, rewarding contributors with incentives.

Business Model

Revenue from gas subscriptions, fertilizer byproducts, and carbon credits. Incentives drive user engagement and waste collection.
